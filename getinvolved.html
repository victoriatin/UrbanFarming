How can I get involved?


We are always looking for volunteers to join us in the Orlando community to help plant, harvest, and survery new garden locations.
If you are interested in becoming a volunteer, please use the contact form at the bottom of this page and send us a message to be added to our volunteer list.
We send out email notifications to our volunteers about the Growlando events coming up that require volunteer help.



Not interested in volunteering but still want to be involved?


We are always accepting donations of gardening materials to help support our community gardens.
See the 'sponser a garden' section below for how to donate and what is accepted as a donation.



Can't volunteer and can't donate but still want to show support to Growlando?


We ask that you help spread the word about Growlando and the impact we are trying to make in the Orlando community.
In addition to spreading the word, check out our resource page to learn more about urban farming.

Follow us on our social media sites to stay updated on what Growlando is doing!

Sponser a garden


If you are wanting to help support the community garden plots, we accept donations of gardening materials such as:


  • Potting soil
  • Plant food
  • Seeds
  • Gardening shears
  • Gardening gloves
  • Trellises

With your donation you will have a garden rock with your name on it displayed in the garden!
