About

Growlando Urban Farming is a non-profit agriculture program that works to provide sustainable and localized food systems in the Greater Orlando area. The introduction of our urban farming program can help reduce negative environmental impacts by promoting sustainable agriculture as well as increase the sense of interconnection within communities. We strive to provide long-term food security as well as improve economic, social, and environmental problems. Let's grow together.
