Featured Post


This past weekend showed great turn out at our community gardening event located in south Orlando. This farm/garden is owned by a private residence and asked for Growlando volunteers to help harvest the growing crops as well as plant new ones. All ages were welcome to join and got to take home a small basket of the vegetables they harvested. The rest of the harvest will be sold at this week's Lake Eola's farmer's market. Be sure to attend the farmer's market to get fresh produce! Thank you again to all of our Growlando volunteers who came out to help!
