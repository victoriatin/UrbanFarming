What is Urban Farming?

Locally grown and consumed produce has been shown to reduce the use of carbon-based fuels due to transportation over long distances and thereby reduces carbon emissions; and the implementation of an urban agriculture ordinance will restore agricultural business to the local economy and bring fresh produce to those areas where the population may not have the means to obtain fresh produce.
