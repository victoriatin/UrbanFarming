What is Urban Farming?

Locally grown and consumed produce has been shown to reduce the use of carbon-based fuels due to transportation over long distances and thereby reduces carbon emissions; and the implementation of an urban agriculture ordinance will restore agricultural business to the local economy and bring fresh produce to those areas where the population may not have the means to obtain fresh produce.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to supply Orlando with an abundance of food for the community and those in need while encouraging the establishment of unused land, getting locals active and outside, and finally creating an economically sustainable program for our city.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be Orlando's most community-centric urban farming program where locals contribute, connect, and benefit from sustainable opportunities that give back to our great city.

About

Growlando Urban Farming is a non-profit agriculture program that works to provide sustainable and localized food systems in the Greater Orlando area. The introduction of our urban farming program can help reduce negative environmental impacts by promoting sustainable agriculture as well as increase the sense of interconnection within communities. We strive to provide long-term food security as well as improve economic, social, and environmental problems. Let's grow together.
